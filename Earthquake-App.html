EARTHQUAKE APP

Android project

Project Description

Project is basically a systematic and fast information transfer principle in earthquakes and other disasters. In the first moments of the earthquake, many systems in our country become active. In this case, thanks to a program that has been programmed before, people can send an emergency notification to the most gathering area and their relatives, with a message that they are good enough to send them to the most gathering area and their relatives. The main point of the project is that it gives information about the information of the people who have been under the rubble and how many people have been in that wreckage. The phones that are under the wreckage are scanned by Bluetooth technology, which devices, hence the people, by scanning their information to the team in the field or, if they are still active.

Application's Features

- People can find earthquake recovery areas in their environment. 
- Together with the people around him, he can inform how many people are under the rubble. 
- With location services, it can help approximate localization. 
- Can receive important information from the authorities. 
- Can give information about his loved ones, friends and authorities about himself.

Included Features

● An emergency button that turns on bluetooth and shuts down unnecessary services in order to preserve battery. Also keeps the screen unlocked to make the phone accessible if the user falls unconscious.
       
●  A bluetooth chat window that allows you to communicate with nearby devices by using only bluetooth. This will be particularly useful because most mobile services won’t be available after an earthquake.     

● A contact database interface that allows you to add and delete contacts to the database. Added contacts can be notified your status instantly via a text message.     

● An earthquake database interface that shows you the past earthquakes.      

● A guide on how to prepare an emergency bag for an earthquake      

● A guide on what to do during an earthquake
